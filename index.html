Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app work?
You set an initial interval between smoke breaks. Each time you press "Smoke", the interval automatically increases by 1 minute, helping you gradually reduce consumption.
Does the app collect my data?
No. All data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
Why do I need to allow notifications?
Notifications are used to remind you when the timer expires. They work even when the app is closed or in the background.
